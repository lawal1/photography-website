Frequently Asked Questions.

Who is teaching The Business of Photography?

The Business of Photography is taught by Bedge, one of Africa's leading wedding photographers and creative business builders. You will also learn from selected industry leaders across business, client management, technical skills, and creative execution.

Is this only for wedding photographers?

No. The programme is for photographers across different niches, including weddings, portraits, events, products, fashion, lifestyle, and commercial work.

Is it only for photographers?

It is mainly built for photographers, but videographers, content creators, and other visual creatives who work with clients on paid projects can also benefit.

I am just starting out. Can I still join?

Yes, if you are serious about photography as more than a hobby and want to move from taking pictures to building a real photography business.

I do not have professional gear yet. Can I join?

Yes. You do not need the best camera before joining, and the programme will help you understand what gear matters and what to prioritise.

Is this a live training or a recorded course?

It is a mix of both: pre-recorded lessons led by Bedge and live online sessions where you can ask questions, get feedback, and learn from industry leaders.

Is there any physical training?

Yes. There will be a physical technical photography training session led by Canon, and it will also be streamed online for students who cannot attend physically.

What if I am not in Lagos?

You can still join. The main programme is online, and the Canon physical training will also be streamed for students outside Lagos.

What if I am not in Nigeria?

You can join from outside Nigeria and access the recorded lessons and live sessions online. Local gear financing or partner benefits may vary by country.

How does the programme work?

After joining, you get access to the community, learning platform, class modules, and support from a success officer who guides you through the programme.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. Installment options are available, and the available payment plans are shown in the payment section.

Can I apply for a scholarship?

Scholarships are only available to people who attended our webinar and meet the scholarship requirements.

Can I pay in another currency?

Yes. International students can pay in another currency, depending on the payment options available at checkout.

Will I learn how to price my services?

Yes. You will learn how to package your work, communicate your value, and price with more confidence.

Will this help me get better clients?

Yes. The programme covers positioning, portfolio proof, client communication, offer structure, and business systems.

How long is the programme?

The Business of Photography runs for 8 weeks with recorded lessons, live training, assignments, and technical support sessions.
